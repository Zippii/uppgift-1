Grim the Shadowreaper

Description

Grim is clad in a dark cowl with a dark hood over his head. Heâ€™s believed to be nothing but bones but no one has ever seen him without his cowl or hood. His weapon is a scythe. Grim is a melee based assassin. Grim is a physical based Criminal that works well with physical damage increasing items, also all of his abilities scales with physical damage.

  • Abilities
  • (Q) Reap: Grim fires his scythe in a straight line dealing damage to enemies hit. Enemies hit gets marked and can be attacked by Grim for additional damage. The mark only procs(processes) once.
  • (W) Flay: Grim spins around with his scythe and all enemies takes damage based on their missing HP. (Less HP, more damage)
  • (E) Shadow Walk: Grim teleports a small distance to target location.
  • (R) Shadowstrike: Grim delivers a powerful blow based on the enemy maximum HP. After the blow is delivered Grim has extra armour penetration against the target.
    [Note that the R and W can be stacked for huge amounts of burst damage.]
