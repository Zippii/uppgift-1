Blaze the Living Flames

Description

Blaze consists of fire and uses his own fire as his weapon. His form is the form of a human guy but his form can change depending on the situation. Blaze is a ranged mage. Blaze is a magic power based criminal which means that he works well with items that grant magical power. All of his abilities scale with magic power too.

    Abilities
  • (Q) Fireball: Blaze fires a fireball at target location, if it hits an enemy it burns them for their maximum HP.
  • (W) Pillar of Fire: Blaze summons a pillar of fire at target location and after 2 seconds it fires up and deals damage to all enemies hit.
  • (E) Fireblast: Blaze shoots out fire from his arms at target location and all enemies hit takes additional damage over time.
  • (R) Bouncing Fireball: Blaze fires a big fireball that bounces 5 times between nearby enemies, enemies hit once gets a burn damage over time while enemies hit several times are slowed for 50% for 2 seconds.
    [Note that R deals the same damage even if it hits the same enemy additional times.]
